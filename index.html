Logo Meaning

Our School Emblem

"Service to Humanity is service to God" — the motto that inspires our foundation.

Foundation

"Service to Humanity is service to God" is the motto of our School emblem and the inspiration for the foundation of the institution.

Pupil

In a polite and disciplined manner the pupil going upward to the path of progress.

River

The flowing river is symbol of knowledge and learning.

Mountain

The high mountain inspires the students to go over the obstacles of life and to reach to its zenith of success.

The Sun

The rising sun symbolizes the 'Guru' that enlightens pupil and the world with the golden rays of knowledge.

Nature

The nature indicates the need of a good and healthy environment for the growth of seeds (Students).
